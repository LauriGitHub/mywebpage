Godrick the Grafted

Godrick the Grafted, also known as Godrick the Golden, is a Demigod Boss in Elden Ring. Bosses are unique and challenging Enemies that can be encountered all over the Lands Between. Shardbearers drop their own unique Great Rune and Remembrance capable of being transformed into powerful Weapons, Spells, Ashes of War, and Talismans for the player. Godrick the Grafted combat information

  • Health: 6,080 HP
  • Defense: 105
  • Stance: 105
  • Parryable: No
  • Is vulnerable to a critical hit after being stance broken
  • Has increased defense during phase transition
  • His Stormcaller attack deflects throwable items like arrows and pots
  • Damage types: Standard, Fire
  • Drops 20 000 runes, Godrick's Great Rune, Remembrance of the Grafted
